Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Hopebridge Foundation

How can I get help from Hopebridge Foundation?

You can reach out to us through the contact form above, call our main office, or visit us in person. Our team will assess your needs and connect you with the appropriate services. We serve all community members regardless of background or circumstances.

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive services including education programs, healthcare support, community development, emergency response, and youth development. Each program is designed to address specific community needs while promoting long-term sustainability and empowerment.

How can I volunteer with Hopebridge?

We welcome volunteers with various skills and time commitments. Visit our volunteer page or contact our volunteer coordinator at info@hopebridgeug.org to learn about current opportunities. We offer both one-time and ongoing volunteer positions.

How are donations used?

85% of all donations go directly to program services, with 10% for administrative costs and 5% for fundraising. We maintain full transparency in our financial reporting and can provide detailed information about how specific donations support our work.

Do you partner with other organizations?

Yes, we actively seek partnerships with other nonprofits, businesses, government agencies, and community groups. If you're interested in partnering with us, please select "Partnership Opportunities" when contacting us, and our development team will get in touch.
