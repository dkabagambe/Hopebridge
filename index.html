Who We Are

Hopebridge Foundation is a humanitarian NGO working across Uganda like Red Cross, dedicated to saving lives, reducing suffering, and protecting human dignity during emergencies and disasters.

We act quickly during emergencies, work in high-risk and disaster-affected areas, collaborate with government and international bodies, and provide essential aid including clean water, sanitation, emergency food distribution, shelter, and medical assistance to communities in need.
